IITH MessMate

Welcome to MessMate,

Our very own Mess Food and Nutrition Tracking App!

Discover a new way to stay healthy and satisfied with our innovative web application. Designed specifically for the students of IITH, our app revolutionizes the way you track your food and nutrition intake in the college mess.



    Key Features

  • Hassle-free Food Logging

    Say goodbye to manual tracking! Our app provides a seamless experience for logging your daily meals, ensuring accurate records without any effort.

  • Personalized Nutrition Insights

    Get ready to unleash the power of data! Our app analyzes your eating habits and provides personalized insights on your nutritional intake, helping you make informed decisions about your diet.

  • Real-time Menu Updates

    No more surprises at the mess! Stay up-to-date with the daily menu, including special dishes and dietary options, ensuring you never miss out on your favorites.

  • Progress Tracking

    Set your goals and track your progress effortlessly. Our app offers visual representations of your eating patterns, helping you stay motivated and on track towards a healthier lifestyle.
